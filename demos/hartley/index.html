The Hartley Approach

A different kind of kitchen company.

We do not sell kitchens from a catalogue. Every Hartley project begins with a conversation about how you live — and ends with a space that could only ever belong to you.

01

Design Without Compromise

Our designers work exclusively on bespoke commissions — which means your project receives undivided attention from concept through to completion. No templates, no showroom standards imposed on your home.

02

Materials That Age Beautifully

Every surface, door, and fitting is specified for longevity as well as appearance. We work with natural stone, solid timbers, and hand-finished lacquers chosen for how they will look in ten years — not just on the day of handover.

03

A Managed Project, Start to Finish

From design approval through to final installation, your project has one point of contact at Hartley. We coordinate design, manufacturing, delivery, and installation — so you do not have to manage a chain of tradespeople.

How It Works

The process is as considered as the result.

We begin with a detailed site survey and a conversation about how you use your kitchen — your routines, your priorities, the things your current kitchen fails to deliver. From that understanding, our designers develop a concept that addresses those specifics, not just the room dimensions.

You will see detailed drawings, sample boards, and finish selections before a single component enters production. Nothing is ordered until you are certain — because a Hartley kitchen is built once, and built to last.

Your Journey

From first conversation to finished kitchen.

1

Initial Consultation

We meet at your home to understand the space, your brief, and your vision. No cost, no commitment — just a considered conversation.

2

Design Development

Our designers present detailed drawings, a material sample board, and a refined proposal tailored to your brief and budget.

3

Production

Your kitchen is crafted by our workshop to your approved specification — a process of approximately eight to ten weeks.

4

Installation & Handover

Our installation team fits and finishes your kitchen to exacting standards. We do not leave until everything is exactly right.

Materials & Finishes

Chosen for beauty and longevity in equal measure.

We source materials that improve with age — timbers that develop a richer patina, stone that deepens in character, brasswork that acquires an honest warmth over years of use. Our specification process is built around longevity, not trend cycles.

Every material we present has been evaluated for how it behaves in a kitchen environment — and we are direct with clients about the maintenance each one requires, because an honest relationship produces a better outcome than one built on flattery.
