Our Approach

The bathroom as the most considered room in the house.

Most rooms are designed around furniture and arrangement. A bathroom is designed around an experience — the quality of light, the temperature of the surface, the silence of a well-sealed room. It is the only space in the house where the material itself is the architecture.

Aura treats every bathroom as a piece of precision interior design. We are not a showroom that specifies from a catalogue. We design the space, specify the materials, and install the result — with the same person responsible for each stage.

What We Do

Three routes to an Aura bathroom.

Whether you are building from scratch, renovating an existing space, or improving a bathroom that never quite worked, there is a service structured for your situation.

01

Full Design & Installation

Our complete service — from the initial consultation and spatial design through to the final installation and snagging. Aura manages every part of the process, including all trades, materials procurement, and quality control. One point of contact. One standard of finish.

02

Design Consultation

A focused design and specification service for clients who have their own contractor but want Aura's expertise in materials, layout, and detailing. We produce a complete specification pack — detailed enough for any competent installer to work from without ambiguity.

03

Material Specification

Expert guidance on materials selection, sourcing, and specification for clients who know what they want to achieve but need Aura's supply relationships and technical knowledge to make the right decisions. Available independently or as part of the design service.

Materials

Every surface chosen for what it does, not just what it looks like.

We work with natural stone quarried from named sources, solid brass hardware from specialist foundries, and timber that has been selected and treated for bathroom conditions without compromising its character. Nothing is specified for aesthetic effect alone — everything performs.

Our material relationships allow us to source stone slabs that match across large wall areas, specify hardware in finishes that are not available through standard trade accounts, and guarantee that what is installed matches what was presented in the design.
