Every Aura project begins with a consultation. We meet at the showroom, discuss your brief in full, and review material samples together. There is no charge for the initial consultation and no obligation to proceed. We would rather have an honest conversation first.

What to Expect

A consultation, not a sales meeting.

We do not hold initial consultations with a prepared proposal or a standard package to present. We come to understand your project — the space, the brief, the way you use the room, and what you want from the result. Everything that follows is shaped by that conversation.

Step 1 Showroom consultation

A private appointment at our Chelsea showroom. Typically one to one and a half hours. We review material samples, discuss the brief in detail, and assess whether Aura is the right studio for your project.

Step 2 Site visit and survey

If we both wish to proceed, we arrange a site visit to survey the space precisely. The survey is the foundation of the design — accurate dimensions, plumbing positions, structural constraints, and natural light conditions.

Step 3 Design and specification

We produce a complete design — layout, materials, fixtures, lighting — and present it with a full specification document and a fixed project cost. Nothing changes after the specification is agreed.

Step 4 Installation

Managed installation by our specialist trades team, with Aura present throughout. Handover takes place when every detail has been reviewed against the specification and signed off.

A Note on Intake

We take on a limited number of projects each year — and we intend to keep it that way.

Aura's model depends on every project receiving the same level of design attention and personal involvement. If our current schedule means we cannot accommodate your timeline, we will tell you at the initial consultation — before you have committed to anything. We do not carry a waiting list for the sake of appearances.

Showroom appointments only

We do not accept walk-in enquiries and we do not hold open showroom days. Every consultation is private and scheduled — because that is the only way to give it the attention it deserves.

Honest from the first meeting

If the project is not right for Aura — whether that is a question of budget, timeline, or scope — we say so at the initial consultation, clearly and without ambiguity. We would rather be direct than waste your time or ours.

Fixed costs, no surprises

Once the specification is agreed, the project cost is fixed. We do not adjust costs after the build has started. Every decision that might affect cost is discussed before the specification is finalised and signed off.
