What Happens Next

A straightforward process from first conversation to finished kitchen.

Step 1 Initial Home Visit

One of our designers visits your home to see the space, understand the brief, and begin a conversation about what you are trying to achieve. This typically takes an hour and carries no obligation.

Step 2 Design Proposal

We present a considered design proposal within three weeks — detailed drawings, a material sample board, and a transparent investment breakdown. Nothing is approximate or indicative.

Step 3 Refinement & Approval

We refine the design until you are certain. Only when you have approved every detail does production begin — typically an eight to ten week process in our Wiltshire workshop.

Step 4 Installation & Handover

Our own installation team fits your kitchen to the same standard as the design and production. We do not hand over until every detail meets the standard we promised at the beginning.

What to Expect

We respect your time and your home.

You will not receive a sales pitch at your initial consultation. You will receive a considered assessment of your space, an honest conversation about what is realistic within your budget, and a clear sense of whether Hartley & Co is the right partner for your project.

No pressure

No obligation at any stage

The initial consultation carries no cost and no commitment. We do not follow up with high-pressure sales calls or time-limited offers. Either we are the right fit, or we are not.

Honest

Direct about what is achievable

If your budget does not align with the level of specification you are hoping for, we will tell you clearly — and we will tell you why. We would rather be honest early than disappoint you later.

Personal

One designer throughout

You will meet the designer who will be responsible for your project at the initial consultation. We do not pass briefs between different members of staff once the relationship has begun.
