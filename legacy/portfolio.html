Premium Kitchen Design & Installation

Hartley & Co Kitchens

Full four-page site — explore now ↗

A luxury kitchen design studio serving homeowners with high-specification briefs and budgets to match — where the website is the first proof of quality.

  • Premium portfolio presentation for bespoke craftwork and materials
  • Consultation-focused contact flow designed to qualify high-value leads
  • Visual hierarchy that communicates quality before a word is read
  • Clear positioning away from mid-market and contractor websites
The Brief

Homeowners investing in a high-specification kitchen need to trust the studio before they book an initial consultation — and that trust is largely built through the website. The challenge was to create a digital presence that communicates craftsmanship, bespoke service, and premium pricing authority without drifting into generic showroom territory. Every design decision was made to shorten the distance between first impression and a booked consultation.

Design Direction

Editorial typography, generous negative space, and a calm, considered layout that reads more like a design studio than a contractor. Materials and finishes are treated as the creative centrepiece — not filed away in a product catalogue. The result is a website that feels as considered as the kitchens themselves.

Landscape Architecture & Garden Design

Verde Garden Design

Full four-page site — explore now ↗

A boutique landscape practice designing considered outdoor spaces for private homeowners and select residential developers who treat the garden as an extension of the interior.

  • Seasonal portfolio presentation built for slow, thoughtful browsing
  • Client journey from first inspiration through to booked consultation
  • Editorial tone that reflects the patience and care of the discipline
  • Premium boutique positioning without losing warmth or approachability
The Brief

Garden design practices occupy an unusual position — too specialist for generic directories, too creative for corporate positioning. Verde's brief was to find the premium boutique middle ground: a website that feels calm, considered, and quietly aspirational. It needed to attract homeowners who see their outdoor space as a long-term investment, not a maintenance contract.

Design Direction

Generous whitespace, organic visual language, and a portfolio structure built to give the work room to breathe. Seasonal photography framed within a layout that moves the visitor naturally from inspiration to enquiry. Soft, confident, and entirely free of the contractor aesthetic that undermines so many design businesses in this sector.

Luxury Bathroom Design & Installation

Aura Bathrooms

Full four-page site — explore now ↗

A specialist bathroom design studio operating at the premium end of the London residential market, where the website must function as the showroom's first room — communicating material quality and design authority before a client steps through the door.

  • Showroom-calibre visual hierarchy for materials, finishes, and collections
  • Clear premium positioning away from mid-market and discount competitors
  • Strong consultation booking flow for high-value project leads
  • Trust architecture designed to drive the showroom visit
The Brief

Luxury bathroom companies operate in a market that spans discount tile retailers to heritage brand showrooms. Aura's challenge was to communicate artisan quality, design exclusivity, and a premium in-showroom experience before the client steps through the door. The website needed to carry that positioning convincingly — spa-toned, architectural, and free of the showroom clichés that undermine most businesses in this space.

Design Direction

Near-white palette, material-forward layout hierarchy, squared architectural buttons that echo the precision of the work itself, and the kind of quiet confidence that signals a business which does not need to shout about its quality. Every surface of the design was treated with the same precision the studio brings to a finished bathroom — controlled detail, generous space, and nothing superfluous.
